BSM

Behavioral Skills and Mindset refer to a person's abilities, attitudes, and beliefs that influence their behavior and interactions with others.
These skills can help determine a person's actions, thoughts and emotions. Many behavioral skills are social attributes that help people connect with others and form relationships.

Extra Support

Extra support for students is crucial because it provides struggling students with the necessary assistance to catch up and stay on track, offers individual attention to clarify confusing concepts, increases engagement and motivation, addresses diverse needs, and ultimately leads to improved academic performance. Whether it's large class sizes, complex material, diverse student backgrounds, learning differences, or language barriers, extra support can help ensure that all students have the opportunity to succeed academically.

Our approach to student support revolves around identifying areas where individuals may require extra help, and providing them with the necessary resources and guidance to enhance their skills and excel academically and professionally. We place significant emphasis on developing interpersonal and intrapersonal skills, with a specific focus on the following areas:

  • Adaptability
  • Teamwork
  • Communication
  • Personal Responsibility
  1. Persistance
  2. Futur orientation
  3. Proactiveness
  4. Growth mindset
